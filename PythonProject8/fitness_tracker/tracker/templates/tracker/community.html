🔥 Why Join a Fitness Challenge?

Fitness challenges help build consistency, accountability, and motivation. Whether you're a beginner or a fitness enthusiast, joining a community-driven challenge can help you:

  • ✅ Stay motivated with structured fitness goals.
  • ✅ Track progress and see measurable improvements.
  • ✅ Engage with others, share achievements, and get inspired.
  • ✅ Build a habit of regular exercise in a fun way.

💪 30-Day Push-Up Challenge

Gradually increase your upper body strength over 30 days.

How It Works:

  • 📅 Day 1: Start with 10 push-ups.
  • 📈 Add 2 more push-ups daily.
  • 🏆 By day 30, achieve 60 push-ups!
